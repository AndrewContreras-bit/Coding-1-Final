Northern Long-eared Bats

Fur and wing membranes are tan, with black ears and black wings: long tail and a wingspan up to 10 inches (25 cm). Look for their long, pointed ears. Also called the Northern Myotis. Northern Long-eared Bats are found in the United States in forested habitats with spruce and pine trees
